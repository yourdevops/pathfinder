What are CI Workflows?

CI Workflows define the build, test, and delivery (package) steps in the CI pipeline for your service. The delivery (package) step defines which artifact is produced after the pipeline run. The type of the artifact defines the available deployment methods.

When assigned, a CI manifest will be generated and pushed to your repository.

This step is optional. You can skip it by selecting "No workflow". The service will be created with Draft status, and you can assign a workflow later from the service's CI tab.
