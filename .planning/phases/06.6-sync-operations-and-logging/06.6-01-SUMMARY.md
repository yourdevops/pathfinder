## Decisions Made
- **trigger field enhancement**: Added trigger field (manual/webhook/scheduled) to StepsRepoSyncLog beyond the original design spec for operational observability
- **SHA skip optimization**: When HEAD SHA matches last_scanned_sha, skip the full scan and record a "skipped" sync log entry
- **Non-blocking branch protection**: Protection check runs but never aborts the scan; result is logged as a warning entry
- **Separate requires_pr and required_reviews rules**: Detects branches that require PRs but allow 0 reviews (self-merge)
- **No entries for unchanged steps**: Only log actual changes to avoid noise in sync entries
